# Privacy

No Shorts No Reels does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal data.

The browser extension runs locally in Safari or Firefox on YouTube and Instagram pages. It hides links and page elements related to YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels, and redirects direct Shorts/Reels URLs back to the corresponding home page.

There is no analytics, tracking, remote logging, account system, or external service used by this project.
